Get Archived!

By Dan_foodz | Main Page

This is a tool made by Dan_foodz to archive any profile. This tool uses Archive.org to archive your profile!




HOW TO USE:

First: Put your USER ID in the text box, you can get your UserID by going to your profile, and copying the number in the URL bar.

Next: You will be sent to a URL, if it's taking to long to load (more then 30 seconds), then refresh the page, and your profiles archive should be there.

If you ever want to get to your profile archive, or anyone elses, just use this URL: danfoodz.github.io/ap?USERIDHERE, and replace USERIDHERE with your, or someone elses UserID.
