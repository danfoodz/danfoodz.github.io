What is The Roblox Archive?

The Roblox Archive is a project made by Dan_foodz to do one thing, to archive as much of Roblox as possible using Archive.org.

How can I be apart of it?

By joining the Everything Dan_foodz Discord, you can request Users, Groups, Games, and Models to be archive with the Archive.org account.

How can I look for archives?

You can go to TheRobloxArchive Archive.org Account, and search uploads to see if you can find anything your looking for, if not, you can send us a archiving request though discord!
